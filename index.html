Ales

Ales include everything from barley wines to the popular india pale ales which contain hops to produce their classic, bitter flavor.

Lagers

One of the most widely consumed styles of beer, lagers span the color palette from lighter, pale pilsners to darker, malty dunkels.

Stouts

Stouts are dark beers that often introduce other ingredients, including chocolate, coffee, and oysters, to create unique flavors.
